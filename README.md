# Customer Reviews and Product Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project analyzes customer review data collected from Kaggle to understand trends in product preferences, engagement, and performance.  
The goal is to visualize the relationship between product prices and average ratings and identify the brands and items delivering the best value for money.

---

## Objectives
- Explore the relationship between product price and customer rating trends.  
- Identify the most preferred brands and best‑rated products.  
- Highlight high‑end products to assess whether higher price points correlate with better ratings.  
- Provide an accessible, interactive dashboard for decision‑makers.

---

## Tools Used
- **Power BI** for interactive visualization and drill‑down analysis.  
- **Kaggle dataset** as the main data source.  
- **Excel/CSV** for data preparation and cleaning.

### Rating vs Price Relationship
- Mid‑priced products received the highest average ratings, showing that customers value a balance between cost and quality.  
- High‑priced products performed well in ratings only when offered by trusted brands.  
- Lower‑priced products showed inconsistent ratings due to quality variations.

### Brand Performance
- A select group of brands consistently ranked among the top 5 in terms of average rating.  
- Newer brands are emerging with competitive prices and quality, suggesting growing market potential.  
- Established brands maintain steady ratings across a range of price points, reflecting strong customer trust.

### Product Insights
| Category | Observation |
|-----------|-------------|
| Top‑Rated Products | Ratings above 4.5 on average |
| Most Expensive Products | Prices above 100 USD with ratings averaging around 4.0 |
| Overall Average Rating | Approximately 4.1 stars |

### Recommendations
- Maintain competitive pricing for mid‑range products that demonstrate strong customer satisfaction.  
- Invest marketing efforts in mid‑price segment brands with proven performance.  
- Examine reasons for inconsistent ratings among cheaper products to improve quality perception.  
- Continue promoting trusted brands at premium price points, emphasizing reliability and product excellence.
